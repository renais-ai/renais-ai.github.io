About & API use

Renais Agency is a digital marketing agency based in Alajuela, Costa Rica. We manage Google Ads accounts for small and medium businesses across Latin America through a single MCC, giving us a centralized view across every client we run.

We're building internal tooling on top of the Google Ads API to automate what used to be manual: pulling performance reports, scanning search terms for negatives, and adjusting bids across accounts — so optimization happens weekly instead of quarterly.

Our approach is pragmatic: we say no to Google features that quietly burn budget, and yes to the ones that consistently earn their place in the ledger.
